Your task is simple!

You, along with another player will test your knowledge on Bible passages!

The passage will be randomly selected for both players! Then... the battle begins!

Both players will have a total of 120 seconds to type everything that they know regarding the passage.

After the 60 seconds have passed, TIMES UP! Both players will get their text analyzed and the more accurate one wins a point!

First player to 3 points WINS!



GOOD LUCK AND HAVE FUN!
